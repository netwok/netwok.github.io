CHATBOT WORKSHOP ON SATURDAY - overview and pre-requisites
With advances in machine learning technology and businesses moving to messaging services to target users, it’s not hard to see why chatbots are taking over. In just one hour, we will go through the process of creating a chatbot, with no presumed knowledge beforehand. I’ll take you through step-by-step, code along style, in creating a chatbot that can gather information from a user, and leave you with the tools and knowledge to create your own. All the tools used in this workshop are free and available on GitHub.
Pre-Requisites (5-10 minutes):
Node - https://nodejs.org/en/download/
BotFramework Emulator - https://github.com/Microsoft/BotFramework-Emulator/releases
JavaScript editor of choice (mines is Visual Studio Code)
